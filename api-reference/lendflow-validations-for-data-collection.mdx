## Prequal Details

### Block ID `prequal`

**Sample Payload**

```json JSON
"prequal": {
    "prequal_requested_amount": 181129.84,
    "prequal_monthly_avg_revenue": "monthly_avg_revenue_1",
    "prequal_time_in_business": "time_in_business_1",
    "prequal_credit_score": "credit_score_1"
},
```
| Field                              | Field ID                    | Validation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
|------------------------------------|-----------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Prequal Funding Request            | `prequal_requested_amount`    | • Numeric <br /> • Greater than 0 <br /> • Less than 999,999,999.99                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Prequal Monthly Revenue            | `prequal_monthly_avg_revenue` | Must be one of <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_1` (0 - 4000) <br /> <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_2` (4001 - 8000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_3` (8001 - 14000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_4` (14001 - 20000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_5` (20001 - 30000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_6` (30001 - 50000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_7` (50001 - 100000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_8` (100001 - 150000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_9` (150001 - 250000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_1`0 (250001 - 500000) <br /> • `monthly_avg_revenue_1`1 (500001 - 1000000) <br /> • monthly_avg_revenue_10 (1000000+) <br /> |
| Customer Reported Time in Business | `prequal_time_in_business`    | Must be one of • `time_in_business_1` (Less than three months) <br /> • `time_in_business_2` (3 - 5 Months) <br /> • `time_in_business_3` (6 - 12 Months) <br /> • `time_in_business_4` (1 - 2 Years) <br /> • `time_in_business_5` (2 - 5 Years) <br /> • `time_in_business_6` (5 - 10 Years) <br /> • `time_in_business_7` (10 Years +) <br /> • `time_in_business_8` (I have not registered a business yet) <br /> • `time_in_business_9` (N/A) <br />                                                                                                                                       |
| Personal Credit Score              | `prequal_credit_score`        | Must be one of • `credit_score_1` (449 or below) <br /> • `credit_score_2` (450 - 499) <br /> • `credit_score_3` (500 - 549) <br /> • `credit_score_4` (550 - 599) <br /> • `credit_score_5` (600 - 649) <br /> • `credit_score_6` (650 - 719) <br /> • `credit_score_7` (720 or above) <br /> • `credit_score_8` (Unsure) <br /> • `credit_score_9` (N/A) <br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
